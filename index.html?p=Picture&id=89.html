Drapey Chain Necklace

This designer necklace was created in 2011. The center bead is pyrite and is flanked by two dyed Serpentinite Jasper semi-precious stones. The pattern is repeated in between white turquoise beads flanked with Swarovski crystal, pyrite and small white agate stones. The silver chain frames each section. The findings are sterling silver.
