Paraline Beach House Rendering

Dimensions: 11" X 14" with additional black matte board (not shown). Prismacolor markers & Prismacolor pencils were used. This hand-drawn rendering was completed in late 2011 in a 3D Drawing and Rendering class. It displays the floor plans of a beach house design. Paraline drawings give a sense of the space from above, showing the walls at a 45 degree angle.
