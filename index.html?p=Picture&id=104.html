Mauve & Tan Metallic 3-Strand Necklace

This lovely 3-strand designer necklace was made in 2010 for my mother-in-law. It is dotted with black/amber glass lampwork beads, golden freshwater pearl disks, hematite, black banded agate and flat agate disks that vary in color from mauve to rich burgundy. The clasp and findings are in a gold finish.
