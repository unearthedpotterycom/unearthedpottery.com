Maho Beach Scene Mural

This is another mural that was painted in the Spring of 2003 while working at a eco-tourist resort in St John, U.S.V.I. The campground is called Maho Bay Campground and at the time I was doing a work exchange program. I worked in the registration office and was allowed to paint murals on the walls. It is painted with acrylic paint. The scene is taken from the beach view that is seen outside of the registration office window. The logo of the campground is in the upper left corner.
