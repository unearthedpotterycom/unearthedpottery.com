Chartreuse Glass Necklace

This 2-strand designer necklace was made in 2011. The centerpiece bead was created by the artist at the Corcoran School of Art and Design, Georgetown campus, in Washington D.C. It also features clear glass lamp-work beads with a slight spiral stripe, large oval yellow jade beads, burgundy Swarovski beads and light gray-blue seed beads. The findings are in sterling silver.
