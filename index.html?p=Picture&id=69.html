Bone Necklace

This necklace was made in the winter 2008. It is made with bone beads retreived from a visit to a small bead shop in Swakopmund, on the coast of Namibia. The bead in the center above the twisted tubular bone bead is a handmade bead created by the artist at the Corcoran School of Art and Design, Georgetown campus, in Washington D.C. Other beads include yellow jade semi-precious stone disks, lampwork glass beads, & wooden beads with cording in leather and findings in sterling silver.
