Mosaic Bowl Detail

This is a glass bowl made by Christian, my friend and guest artist at the Maho Bay Glassblowing studio in 2006. He gave it to me since it wasn't able to be sold due to a crack in the inner ring. The crack inspired me to layer a colorful glass mosaic on top, contained within the two ring rims incorporated into the piece of art.
Mosaic Bowl Detail2

I worked at Lisa's studio, Syzygy, on St John, to create my first completely glass mosaic. The colorful, stylized waves in it imitate the the waves at the bays of St John.
