Sunset Mural

This mural was painted in the Spring of 2003 while working at a eco-tourist resort in St John, U.S.V.I. The campground is called Maho Bay Campground and at the time I was doing a work exchange program. I worked in the registration office and was allowed to paint murals on the walls. It is painted with acrylic paint. The image is a characterization of a scene from a photo taken from a sunset sail from a sailboat frequently sailing out of Maho Bay, Pepper.
