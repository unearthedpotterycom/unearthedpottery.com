Round Tin Necklace

This designer necklace was created in 2010. The embossed and antiqued tin pendant is layered with a tiered bead design. It includes burgundy, red and pacific colored Swarovski crystal elements as well as ceramic beads glazed with a colorful floral design. The necklace features a white ribbon and a silver clasp.
