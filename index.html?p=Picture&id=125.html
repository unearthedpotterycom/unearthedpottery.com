Kitchen Rendering

Dimensions: 11" X 14" with additional black matte board (not shown). This hand-drawn rendering was completed in late 2011 in a 3D Drawing and Rendering class. Prismacolor markers & Prismacolor pencils were used. This drawing is intended to express the finishes and materials suggested in this design, such as Carrara marble countertops and backsplash and hardwood floors.
