Coral & Black 3-Strand Necklace

This artistic, 3-strand designer necklace was made in summer 2012. The bead of emphasis is a round nautilus shell back-filled with red-orange resin. The strands consist of three different kinds of coral: branch fire coral, salmon colored round coral, and lighter colored oval coral nuggets. They also contain light pink shell chips, wooden beads and black tubular seed beads. The toggle is matte black with black rhinestones.

This necklace would be considered "Beach Elegance".
