3-Strand Asymmetrical Necklace

This designer necklace was made in 2011. The toggle and findings are in antique copper while the main beads are comprised of freshwater pearls and black & amber lamp-work glass beads. Notice the toggle clasp is off to the side in the front. The colors of the seed beads range from an aubergine to mauve to a golden amber.
