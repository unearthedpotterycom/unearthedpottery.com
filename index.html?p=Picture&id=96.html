Elephant Charm Necklace

This designer necklace was created in 2010. The chaotic assortment of seed beed strands has been attached is cleverly fastened in the front with a large antique brass clasp,with is also used to hang the pendant. The antique brass pendant was discovered in a small shop in Swakopmund on the Skeleton Coast of Namibia, Africa. It is layered with ebony bone carved elephant from Swakop flanked by beads of black and white banded agate.
