Malachite Necklace

This necklace was made in 2010. Using a combination of malachite and azurite this necklace is interesting and beautiful. The center portion spaces out the various sizes and shapes of semi-precious stones with white agate stones and silver seed beads. There are also cobalt blue square accent beads. The chain is in silver and the clasp and findings are sterling silver.
