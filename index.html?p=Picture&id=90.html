Egyptian White Turquoise Necklace

This one-of-a-kind necklace was made in 2011. It has been constructed from vintage earring chandeliers retrieved from a small bead shop in Swakopmund, on the African coast of Namibia. It also includes pyrite, white turquoise and crackled black agate semi precious stone beads. The findings are in antique bronze.
