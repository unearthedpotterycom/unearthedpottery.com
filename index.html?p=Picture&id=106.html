Four Strand Y Necklace

This four strand designer necklace was created in 2011. It features an all-over pattern of pacific and pink colored Swarovski crystals and a muted palette of agate semi-precious stone roundels. The agate palette varies from mottled mauves to rust and verdant greens. The findings and clasp are featured in sterling silver.
